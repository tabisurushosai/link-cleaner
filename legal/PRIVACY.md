# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

## Overview
This extension, "link-cleaner", is designed with a strong focus on privacy.

## Data Collection and Communication
- **No External Communication**: This extension does not communicate with any external servers. All processing is done locally on your machine.
- **No Data Collection**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or the URLs you clean.
- **Local Storage**: Any configuration or preferences you set are stored locally within your browser's extension storage and are never uploaded to the cloud.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
